Accommodations

Cigarral de las Mercedes

Our venue has a limited number of on-site rooms. Follow the booking steps below to see prices and reserve.

Steps to book:

1. Visit https://cigarraldelasmercedes.com

2. Enter the dates of your stay

3. In the promotional code section, enter the code "AmandayDavid". We have a discounted rate from October 12 to 14.

Note that some Classic rooms can accommodate up to 3 people. To include an additional bed or a cot, please make a note in the observations box when you finish your reservation, or contact the hotel at reservas@cigarraldelasmercedes.com.
